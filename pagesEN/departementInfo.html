Departments at IUT


The teaching of computer science at the IUT makes a large part of the professional dimension while retaining fundamental components; the student thus acquires know-how but also knowledge, of the basic concepts and methods of work that he will be able to put to use in professional.

The DUT, university and professional diploma (level L2 of the LMD scheme, 120 European credits):

The holders of the DUT in computer science are senior technicians, able to participate in the design, the realization and implementation of computer systems. They can, however, if they wish continue their studies at the end of this degree.

The Professional License "Computer and Software Systems" (SIL), L3 degree in the LMD scheme (Bachelor's degree, 180 European credits): these pages present the professional licenses proposed in the departments iut computer.

The holders of the SIL license are specialists capable of participating in a project of software development and, ultimately, take full responsibility for it.

Validation of achievements:

It is also possible to have your experience (professional or otherwise) recognized in order to obtain A degree, a title or certificate of professional qualification. You can visit the government website concerning validation acquired experience (VAE).
