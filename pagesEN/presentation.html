IUT presentation


The computer science departments of the IUTs of France train students in computer science and work in team to insert them into professional life.

They offer a human-sized training, supervised by teachers available, as well as by experienced professionals who provide students with hands-on, hands-on experience in computing.

Teaching in computer science (DUT or Professional License) is based on 3 axes, each of requiring them specific qualities and skills:

  • Analysis and design:
Preliminary work with the client to specify their needs and expectations and the design of the schema of a computer processing.

Communication, openness, analytical skills and synthesis ...
  • Algorithmic and programming: Effective implementation of processing until coding.

    Logical spirit, technical precision ...
  • System architecture and networks: How a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...).

    Curiosity, patience ...

    To these targeted courses are added half of the subjects of general education (Mathematics, Economics and Management, English and Communication, ...). This gives the formation an appearance multidisciplinary.
    In addition, the evaluation is not only on the theory but also on situation, via tutored projects and an internship in a company, validating knowledge and skills acquired during the training.
