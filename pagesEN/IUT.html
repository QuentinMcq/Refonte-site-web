University Institutes of Technology


University Institutes of Technology in France are spread throughout the country and are among from 115, which ensures a strong anchoring in the local and regional economic environment. Meetings between directors Department of IUTs ensure the national coherence of education.

The IUT has a quality of university education recognized and acclaimed by companies for more than 40 years, thanks to the introduction of programs adapted to professional reality and the creation of diplomas answering the emergence of new professions.
Training covering most sectors (secondary and tertiary) and offering the collaboration of professional make the strength and success of IUTs, and actually allow students to find work in the 6 month following graduation.

This diversification of diplomas and training leads to setting up pedagogical modes different, suitable for a larger number of students and professionals wishing to resume studies or do a validation of acquired experience (VAE).

In addition, IUTs, in partnership with foreign universities, propose abroad, as well as internships.
