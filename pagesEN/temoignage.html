Testimony of Damien Lefebvre



His baccalaureate S in pocket, Damien Lefebvre spent a few months on the benches of the university, before reorienting himself, investigation a more framed training. His academic experience having revealed to him a taste for computing, he decides to apply to the IUT of Villetaneuse to prepare a DUT Computer Science option Software Engineering in 2002.

The supervision proposed by passionate professors, the acquisition of rigorous working methods have made him to complete his training via the Professional License Computer Systems and Software option Engineering Software, proposed by the same IUT.

"The knowledge and skills developed by three years of study of the algorithmic, the bases of data, but also accounting aspects of the business world, were the key that allowed me to be recruited by a French IT service company, after 4 months of end-of-year internship. "

For a year and a half now, the young Programmer Analyst has been putting his skills to the service of customers among which are renowned banking institutions, and in the future wish to move towards "Consulting".
